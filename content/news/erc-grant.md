The BALZER LAB has been awarded an **ERC Starting Grant** to study cellular adaptation mechanisms in the human kidney.

The **[project]({{< relref "research" >}})** will combine single-cell, spatial, and epigenetic approaches to understand renal regeneration and maladaptation.
